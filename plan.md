Initial plan for process

- Minimal integration from the HR side, no need to provide any information besides a link to the job post
- Minimal integration to client website, can work with as little as a link that brings candidates to our platform with the custom screening chat
- Tailored for technical jobs, software, data, etc
- Screens for all technical skills, critical thinking skills, and behavioral skills
- Customizable so clients can specify on something they want to focus on, although it is advised that prescreening is more general and more focused on not missing those smart indivduals that don't make it through the usual application process, but also filter out those that have good looking resumes but lack actual technical knowledge or critial thinking, also helps candidates who may be smart but can get nervous during phone screening, high screen bot scores can indicate these people

Experience:
- Client signs up
- Client creates a Job Bot
- Client provides the link to the job posting
- Our platform takes the link, parses the post for company description, job description, requirements, skills, etc etc any relevant information for the bot to have context
- Based on the contents of the job post, AI bot determines a set of questions to evaluate candidates on their technical skills, critial thinking skills, behavioral skills, and background/experience
- Client is provided a link that they can simply add to their applicant platform or send to candidates via email
- When candidates finish an application, they may either be prompted to use the link in the candidate portal per the clients website, or they may receive an email with the link
